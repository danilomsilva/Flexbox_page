Limerick, Ireland

8.6
6 votes

Once upon a time there was an Irish city ... A lot of people think of a malicious poem when they hear the word Limerick, but in fact, the city inspires much more than the well-known giggles. Medieval castles and churches give the city a grandiose atmosphere, while the pubs are filled with locals and lively students who always want another pint of Guinness. The Hunt Museum has a collection of pieces from the most influential and important artists in history, while the Foynes Flying Boat Museum will delight visitors of all ages with its extravagant flying machines.
